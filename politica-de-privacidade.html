Privacy Policy for Speak Up

Last Updated: June 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Speak Up! This Privacy Policy explains how Speak Up ("I," "me," "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit my website at https://speakup-4ve.pages.dev/ (the "Website") and use its features. This includes when you take our motivation quiz, or use links to contact me via WhatsApp or schedule appointments through Google Calendar. Your privacy is important to me, and I am committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Who I Am (Data Controller)

Speak Up is a service provided by Luiz Felipe Angelo Miranda, a freelance English professor based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. For the purposes of data protection law, particularly the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD), I am the controller of the personal data collected through this Website.

3. What Information I Collect

I collect certain personal information from you so I can provide my services and you can get the most out of your experience with Speak Up. This includes:

Information you give me directly:

  • Your Name
  • Your Email Address
  • Your Phone Number (including for WhatsApp communication)
  • Your responses to the motivation quiz on the Website (which helps me understand your learning interests).
  • Information you provide when scheduling trial lessons or other appointments via my Google Calendar link.

Information for future newsletters (if you choose to subscribe):

  • Your Email Address (and your Name, if you provide it for personalization).

Information I plan to collect (this section will be updated when these features are active):

  • Website Usage Data: When I implement Google Analytics or similar tools, I may collect information like your IP address, browser type, which pages you visited on my site, how long you spent there, and the website you came from.

4. How I Collect Your Information

I collect your information in the following ways:

  • When you voluntarily fill out a contact form on my Website or directly email or message me.
  • When you participate in the motivation quiz available on the Website.
  • When you click on links that direct you to my WhatsApp for communication or to my Google Calendar for scheduling, and you subsequently provide information through those platforms.
  • (Future Addition) When you use the Website, some information may be collected automatically using cookies and other tracking technologies (like Google Analytics).

5. How I Use Your Information

I use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your questions and inquiries about my English classes.
  • Communicate with you via WhatsApp, email, or phone.
  • Schedule trial lessons and other appointments using Google Calendar.
  • Understand your motivations and learning needs (based on your quiz responses) so I can provide you with relevant information and a tailored experience.
  • Generate leads and facilitate bookings for trial lessons, helping me connect with students like you.
  • (Future Addition, with your explicit consent) Send you newsletters with English learning tips, Speak Up news, and information about my classes and offers.
  • (Future Addition, with Google Analytics) Analyze how visitors use my Website so I can improve its content, design, and the services I offer.

I am committed to using your information only for the purposes described here or for purposes that are compatible with these.

6. Legal Basis for Processing Your Information (LGPD Compliance)

I process your personal information based on the following legal grounds as set out by the LGPD:

  • Your Consent: For most of the information I collect – such as when you fill out a contact form, take the quiz, or (in the future) sign up for a newsletter – I do so based on your clear and freely given consent. You provide this consent by voluntarily offering your information for these specified purposes.
  • Legitimate Interests: (Future Addition) For website analytics (like Google Analytics), I may process data based on my legitimate interest in understanding and improving my website and services. I will always ensure this is balanced with your rights and freedoms.

You can withdraw your consent at any time for activities where consent is the legal basis.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Your trust is important, and I do not sell your personal information to third parties.

However, to provide my services and operate my Website, I use some third-party platforms:

  • WhatsApp: If you choose to contact me via the WhatsApp link on my Website, your communication and any data shared will be subject to WhatsApp's privacy policy.
  • Google Calendar: If you use my Google Calendar link to schedule a trial lesson or appointment, your interaction and data shared with Google Calendar are subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • (Future Addition) Google Analytics: I plan to use Google Analytics to help me understand how my Website is being used. This service is provided by Google, and their privacy policy would apply to the data they collect.

I recommend you review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand their data practices.

8. Data Storage and Security

I take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, or disclosure. The information you provide may be stored on password-protected devices and secure cloud-based services. While I strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Data Retention

I will keep your personal information only for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. For example:

  • If you inquire about classes, I'll keep your information to manage our communications and for a reasonable period in case you decide to enroll later.
  • (Future Addition) If you subscribe to my newsletter, I'll keep your email address until you unsubscribe.

10. Your Rights Under LGPD

As someone whose data is processed in Brazil, you have specific rights under the LGPD regarding your personal information. These include the right to:

  • Confirm that I am processing your personal data.
  • Access the data I hold about you.
  • Correct any incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
  • Request anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary or excessive data, or data processed in noncompliance with the LGPD.
  • Request the portability of your data to another service or product provider (where applicable).
  • Request the deletion of personal data processed with your consent (except in cases where retention is legally permitted).
  • Be informed about public and private entities with which I have shared your data (as outlined in Section 7).
  • Be informed about the possibility of not providing consent and the consequences of such denial.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the details provided in the "How to Contact Me" section below.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

(This section will be more relevant and may need expansion once Google Analytics is implemented.)

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website.

Currently, my Website has limited use of cookies. However, I plan to use Google Analytics, which uses cookies to collect information about how visitors use the Website. This information helps me understand user activity (like which pages are most popular) and improve the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Website will generally be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

You can usually manage or disable cookies through your internet browser settings.

12. Children's Privacy

My English classes for children are marketed to and arranged with parents or legal guardians. I do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without verifiable parental consent.

If you are a parent or guardian and you provide information about your child (such as their name or age for class suitability), you do so with the understanding that this information will be used solely for the purpose of discussing and providing English classes for your child. If you believe your child has provided personal information to me without your consent, please contact me immediately so I can take appropriate steps.

13. Links to Other Websites

My Website may contain links to other websites (like WhatsApp and Google Calendar, as mentioned, and potentially others in blog posts or resources). This Privacy Policy only applies to my Website. If you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in my practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. If I make changes, I will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how I am protecting your information.
